Welcome to the Shot Lot

Did your friend drink one too many of your beers? Did the no-wake get crazier than expected? Whatever the reason is, we've got you covered.


Convienence on the Susquehanna

The Shot Lot was created to help you quickly get back to your friends on the water without leaving the water to go to a liquor store. We created a system where you can order your alcohol on your phone before arrival or just order when you get here!

We know alcohol and the sun can wear you down as well but, Don't worry! We have a dine-in service to get you in the shade and refueled. Our menu can be found by clicking on the Food section.
